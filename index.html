About Me

Hi! Thank you for checking out my portfolio.

Since 2015, I’ve been working as a user experience and product designer. In that time, I worked on large web projects at agencies and SaaS products at early-stage startups.

As a young designer, I was always intimidated by code. However, as I grew and gained experience, my interest and curiosity in software development ballooned. Finally, in the last few years of my design career, I started yearning for the ability to go into a codebase and add things or make changes myself, but I didn’t have the skills.

Midway through 2021, I decided to make a change and become a developer. So, I took some time off and focused on learning everything I could about web development. Through a program at Thinkful, I learned a ton. However, I know it’s only the beginning of my journey, and I have so much more to learn.

My passion for technology burns strong, and I have a deep desire to be a part of a team that builds fantastic products. At this time, I’m seeking that first opportunity, so if you like what you’ve seen, please don’t hesitate to reach out!
